# City Heart Tours - Website Redesign Rules

**CRITICAL**: You are working on the City Heart Tours website redesign. You MUST follow these guidelines strictly to preserve the brand's unique selling proposition and avoid generic "corporate" travel templates.

## Brand & Visual Direction
1. **Photography-led, not stock-led**: Use real imagery of Cape Breton (Skyline Trail, Cape Smokey, Fortress, Big Fiddle) rather than generic stock photos.
2. **Warm, Human Color Palette**: Use coastal blues/greens paired with a warm accent (amber/rust) echoing Acadian/Gaelic craft culture. Avoid generic "travel-tech" blue-and-white.
3. **The Face of the Brand**: Jessie's photo/name must appear near every booking CTA (not just the About page). The brand voice is first-person ("I'll take you to...") and personal.
4. **Signature Visuals**: Use simple illustrated route maps for itineraries rather than long prose.

## Information Architecture
Follow this structure:
- **Tours**: Grouped by duration/intent, not alphabetically.
  - Quick Taste (1-4 hrs): Sydney City Drive, Louisbourg Fortress
  - Half-to-Full Day (5-7 hrs): Ingonish & Green Cove, Bell Museum + Mini Cabot
  - The Full Cabot Trail (flagship, 8-10 hrs)
  - Private & Custom: Bell Museum + Highland Village, Sydney Lakes & Lighthouse
- **Meet Your Guide**: Jessie's story.
- **Cruise Passengers**: Dedicated landing page.
- **Reviews**: Live-pulled TripAdvisor feed.
- **FAQ / Good to Know**: Vehicle size, guide-vs-driver promise, clear refund/weather policy.
- **Book Now / Contact**: Must feature a WhatsApp click-to-chat option.

## Tour Page Template Requirements
Every tour page MUST include:
1. **Sticky Price + Reserve CTA**: Show both per-person AND per-group prices where relevant (e.g., "$830/group as low as ~$138/person for 6").
2. **Visual Itinerary**: Numbered stop-by-stop with map and photos, not just text.
3. **Explicit Vehicle Promise**: Photo of vehicle + stated max capacity (e.g., "private tour, max 6 guests, seatbelts for all").
4. **Explicit Guide Promise**: "Your driver is also your guide — English-fluent, Cape Breton local".
5. **Inline Policies**: Weather and cancellation policy directly on the page, not buried in footer. (Highlight: "Free cancellation up to 24 hours").
6. **Contextual Reviews**: Reviews specific to that tour pulled in directly below the itinerary.
7. **Cross-sells**: "Short on time? Try the 4-hour Louisbourg tour instead".

## Psychological Conversion Guidelines
- **Authority**: Lead with the 4.5★/54-review TripAdvisor badge and "#2 of 26 Sydney tours".
- **Social Proof**: Use concrete numbers ("203 travelers have taken our Cabot Trail route").
- **Scarcity/Urgency**: Use real seasonal windows and real capacity limits (max 6 guests). NEVER use fake countdown timers or deceptive scarcity.
- **Pricing Anchor**: Order tours from the flagship ($830) down to cheaper options to make them feel accessible.

**When working on this codebase, always refer back to these rules before creating or modifying components.**
